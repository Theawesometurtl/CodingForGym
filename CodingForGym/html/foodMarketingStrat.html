Food Marketing Strategies

Posititive Association

There are many methods of positive association, where big brands try to associate their brand with positive ideas to increase the likelyhood you will buy their products. Below are a few methods of positive association.




Apealing to Your Senses

Most food advertisements have some aspect of this in them, the giant bite into an overflowing big mac, the telltale crack and sizzle of opening a can of pop, or some other slow-motion shot of food floating through the air. This is an obvious advertising tactic that works. Whenever I see a poster for fast food, my mouth waters thinking about how it must taste. Advertisements like this can be misleading as to how good the food actually is.



Things That Have Nothing to Do With the Brand



Celebrity Influencers

Sometimes companies will pay a celebrity to endorse and promote their product. This works in several ways. Having a celebrity support them makes them look better in the eyes of the public, as long as the celebrity is liked. Also, celebrities are often seen as role models, so if you want to want to become more like a specific celebrity, you might also want to drink the same pop they drink, or eat the same donuts they eat. You like Justin Bieber, so you also like Tim Hortons.
