Big companies use many strategies to get you to consume their products, ranging from creating exciting advertisements that appeal to your senses, to placing their product in a frequently used part of a store. If humans were rational we wouldn't fall prey to any marketing strategies, but unfortunately humans aren't rational, and everyone, including you, are influenced by the flashy ads and product placement

Learning about food marketing is important because understanding the ways big companies get you to consume different foods can help you recognize when these tactics are working on you or others. It can help you make informed food choices that are best for you.
